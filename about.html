The School Head

The School Head

The school Head, Mallam Muhammad Shaba Bukari, is an indigene of Bukari in Lavun Local Government Area of Niger State. He began his education at Makama Primary School, Minna (1991–1996), proceeded to secondary school at the College of Art and Islamic Studies (CAIS), Minna (1997–2002), and then earned a Diploma in Art from the Justice Fati Lami Abubakar College of Art and Islamic Studies, Minna (2002–2004). He later attended Niger State College of Education, where he received an Associate Certificate in Education (2007–2008), before enrolling at Al-Hikmah University in Ilorin, Kwara State, for a B.A. in Islamic Studies. Currently, he is pursuing a Master’s degree in Arabic at IBB University, Lapai..

Mallam Muhammad Shaba received his early Qur’anic education at Alhaji Makanta School, Bukari, and later studied under Shaykh Muhammad Isah Kutigi (R.T.A), covering various fields of Islamic knowledge. He is happily married with three wives and blessed with twelve children, both sons and daughters. Professionally, he has experience in teaching and Sharia law, having served in several Sharia court divisions within Niger State. Presently, he is the Principal Registrar I at Mariga Sharia Court.
